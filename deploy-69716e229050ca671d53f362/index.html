Common Questions

Is this just a wrapper for ChatGPT?

No. While we utilize LLMs for reasoning, our systems are complex software architectures that integrate directly with your database, CRM, and internal tools. They have memory, state, and specific permissions designed for your workflow.

How secure is my data?

Extremely. We build single-tenant architectures. Your data does not commingle with other clients. We use industry-standard encryption and never use your data to train public models.

What happens if the AI makes a mistake?

Our systems include "Human-in-the-Loop" (HITL) modes. For sensitive tasks, the agent drafts the work and waits for approval in your custom portal. As trust builds, you can increase autonomy.

Do I own the code?

For Enterprise plans, yes. For our standard monthly plans, we grant you a perpetual usage license for the duration of the subscription, managing all hosting and maintenance for you.
